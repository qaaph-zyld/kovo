## THE BRUTAL QUESTIONS

Ask after EVERY component:

1. **What makes this look AI-generated?**
2. **Where does this resemble templates?**
3. **What would a senior designer change?**
4. **If the logo was removed, is this recognizable?**
5. **What's the ONE action users should take?**

## DESIGN BY PERSONALITY

### Sharp/Technical (Engineer)
- Radius: 0-4px
- Spacing: Tight
- Typography: Monospace accents
- Color: Limited, high contrast
- Motion: Minimal, functional

### Premium/Minimal (Editor)
- Radius: 4-8px
- Spacing: Generous
- Typography: Large contrast
- Color: Restrained
- Motion: Subtle, purposeful

### Bold/Disruptive (Agitator)
- Radius: Varied, sharp
- Spacing: Dense
- Typography: Aggressive
- Color: High contrast
- Motion: Intentional

### Friendly/Approachable (Host)
- Radius: 8-16px
- Spacing: Comfortable
- Typography: Clear, readable
- Color: Warm
- Motion: Helpful
