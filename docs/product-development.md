## üîÑ Shared Components Strategy

### Interchangeable Parts
- **Backrest Panels**: Same 3 designs work on chair AND bench
- **Connection System**: Identical M8/M10 hardware across all products
- **Wood Finishes**: Consistent wood species and finish across collection
- **Design Language**: Single LINEA motif (subtle arc) across all products

### Benefits
- **Production Efficiency**: Same components, multiple products
- **Inventory Management**: Fewer unique parts to stock
- **Customer Experience**: Mix-and-match customization options
- **Service Revenue**: Replaceable parts create ongoing sales

## üì¶ Transport & Packaging Strategy

### Flat-Pack Design Goals
- **Volume Reduction**: 60-80% smaller than assembled pieces
- **Damage Prevention**: No metal-on-metal contact, protected wood surfaces
- **Assembly Simplicity**: 15-45 minutes maximum, single tool

### Packaging BOM (Per Unit)
- **Euro-palette** for sets, cardboard boxes for individual pieces
- **3-5mm foam** on all contact points
- **Cardboard corner protectors** for sharp edges
- **Stretch film** + packing tape
- **Hardware Kit**: Allen key + all bolts + spare parts + QR instructions

### Transport Models
- **B2C**: Courier for individual pieces, pallet delivery for sets
- **B2B**: Full pallets (4-6 chairs + 1-2 tables) for restaurants/hotels
- **Local Pickup**: 5-10% discount for Loznica pickup

## üé® Design Direction: "Urban Minimal"

### Design Rules
1. **Zero spirals** - Single signature motif max: subtle arc or V-line
2. **Thin metal + warm wood** contrast for premium feel
3. **Consistent metal profile** across all products (25√ó25mm tube + 30√ó6mm bar)
4. **Uniform leg finish** (rubber cap + discreet leveler)

### Aesthetic Goals
- **Modern but not cold** - warmth from wood, precision from metal
- **Premium without ornate** - clean lines, thoughtful details
- **Cohesive collection** - all pieces look designed together

## üß™ Prototyping & Testing Plan

### Week 1: Chair Prototype
1. **Build one chair** using modular design
2. **Test 10+ assembly/disassembly cycles**
3. **Stacking test** - verify 6-chair stack stability
4. **Weight test** - 150kg person load test
5. **Transport simulation** - shake, drop, stack test

### Week 2: Transport Test
1. **Pack 6 chairs flat** - measure actual space vs current
2. **Simulate delivery** - damage assessment after transport
3. **Cost analysis** - hardware + packaging materials
4. **Assembly verification** - first-time user test

### Week 3: Cost & Production Analysis
1. **Per-unit hardware cost** - bolts, pins, tools
2. **Labor impact** - modular vs traditional assembly
3. **Packaging cost** - materials vs damage reduction
4. **Transport savings** - volume reduction analysis

## üö® Critical Success Factors

### Must-Have for Launch
1. **Structural integrity** - no flex, no squeaking, premium feel
2. **Assembly reliability** - consistent 15-minute assembly
3. **Transport safety** - zero damage in flat-pack testing
4. **Visual cohesion** - collection looks designed together

### Red Flags to Monitor
- **M8 bolts undersized** for heavy wrought iron application
- **Welded nuts failing** under repeated stress
- **Paint thickness affecting** bolt clearances
- **Warped steel frames** preventing proper alignment
