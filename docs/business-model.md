### Geographic Focus (Phase 1)
- **Primary**: Serbia (Belgrade, Novi Sad, Ni≈°, Kragujevac)
- **Secondary**: Regional cities (Subotica, Novi Pazar, Kraljevo)
- **Expansion**: Balkans (Croatia, Bosnia, Montenegro) - Year 2

### Delivery Models
| Order Size | Delivery Method | Timeline | Cost |
|------------|-----------------|----------|------|
| Individual items | Courier service | 2-3 days | Included in price |
| Small sets (1-2 items) | Pallet delivery | 5-7 days | 800 RSD |
| Large sets (3+ items) | Dedicated transport | 7-10 days | 1,500 RSD |
| B2B orders | Scheduled delivery | 14-21 days | Negotiated |

### Transport Optimization
- **Flat-pack design**: 65% volume reduction vs assembled
- **Stackable chairs**: 6 units per shipping space
- **Pallet efficiency**: 4-6 sets per standard pallet
- **Damage prevention**: Custom packaging, <1% damage rate
- **Regional hubs**: Distribution centers for faster delivery

### Packaging Strategy
- **Standardized boxes**: Branded, reusable where possible
- **Hardware kits**: Labeled bags with all necessary components
- **Assembly instructions**: QR codes linking to video guides
- **Protection**: Foam inserts, corner protectors, stretch film
- **Sustainability**: Recyclable materials, minimal waste
