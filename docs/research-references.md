## üéØ Key Insights Summary

### What Works in Market
1. **M8/M10 bolts** - Industry standard for furniture assembly
2. **Single tool assembly** - Allen key approach proven successful
3. **Stackable design** - Most effective transport solution
4. **Shared components** - Reduces costs, increases perceived value
5. **Premium positioning** - Modular doesn't mean cheap

### What to Avoid
1. **Complex assembly** - Keep under 45 minutes
2. **Proprietary tools** - Standard tools only
3. **Too many SKUs** - Start with 3-4 hero products
4. **Cheap finishes** - Invest in proper surface preparation
5. **Ignoring B2B** - Restaurant/hotel market loves modular

### Design Principles to Adopt
1. **Clean lines** - Modern but warm aesthetic
2. **Material contrast** - Steel + wood premium feel
3. **Consistent profiles** - Same tubes/bars across products
4. **Hidden fasteners** - Premium appearance
5. **Intuitive assembly** - Parts only fit one way
